About Me

I'm an open source contributor, a CentOS Project board member, a sysadmin, a developer, and an advocate for building better quality tech. I've been an Oil consultant, a defense consultant, and am now with Red Hat. I've been working in open source since 1999, and a member of the CentOS Project since 2004.

These days I spend the majority of my time working on enabling arm64/aarch64 based server software, focusing on IoT development and speaking about how we as an industry can improve.
